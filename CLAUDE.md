# CLAUDE.md

## Urban Cutz Design System

Project: Urban Cutz Barbershop - Los Angeles
Stack: Astro static site, no JavaScript frameworks

### Brand Goals
- Build a polished static website for a local barbershop in Los Angeles.
- Position the site as a conversion-focused portfolio piece for local businesses.
- Prioritize clear booking and calling actions over decoration.

### Color Palette
- Background: #F5F0EB (warm off-white)
- Accent: #1E6FBF (Dodgers blue)
- Text: #1A1A1A (near-black)
- Surface: #FFFFFF (white cards)
- Muted: #6B6B6B (secondary text)

### Typography
- Headlines: Bebas Neue via Google Fonts, all-caps, bold
- Body: Inter via Google Fonts, clean and readable

### Design Rules
- Mobile-first always.
- No JavaScript frameworks.
- No glassmorphism.
- No dark backgrounds.
- No floating orbs or decorative visual effects.
- Every section must serve conversion: book or call.
- Click-to-call and click-to-book are the highest-priority elements.
- Use real content only; no fake stats and no fake brand logos.
- Use clean white cards with subtle drop shadow on a cream background.
- Include a sticky mobile bottom bar with Call and Book buttons always visible.

### Page Sections (in order)
1. Nav - logo left, hours center, Book Now button right
2. Hero - headline, address, phone, two CTAs, photo right
3. Trust bar - Google rating, location, walk-ins welcome
4. Services - clean price cards
5. About - shop story, human copy
6. Gallery - 6 photo grid, no carousel
7. Reviews - 3 Google review cards
8. Booking CTA - full-width blue section
9. Footer - address, hours, map, phone

### Content Direction
- Keep the tone warm, local, and trustworthy.
- Write copy that feels human and specific to a real barbershop.
- Favor direct calls to action over vague marketing language.
- Make the site feel useful to someone deciding whether to book immediately.

### Implementation Notes
- Keep the site fully static and lightweight.
- Focus on clarity, readability, and fast load times.
- Preserve a simple, editorial layout with strong hierarchy and clear conversion points.
